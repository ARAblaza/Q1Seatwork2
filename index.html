CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE


Description
KAPITAN TIAGO
A wealthy Filipino. Despite being native-born, Kapitan Tiago keeps close ties with members of the Catholic Church, however instead of respecting the religion, he uses his connections to join in committing racist attrocities against his own people.
PADRE DAMASO
Even after living among the natives of San Diego for 20 years, Padre Damaso uses his power as a priest of the church to ruin the lives of anyone he dislikes. He's grown no attachment to the citizens around him, raising taxes and orchestrating the deaths of all those who try to take any action.
MARIA CLARA
Here's a plot twist for you: Not the daughter of Kapitan Tiago, but was actually fathered by Padre Damaso.
Being the supposed "daughter of Kapitan Tiago", she is a woman of high standing. She planned to marry Crisostomo, but was unable to after he was excommunicated from the church.
PADRE SALVI
A younger priest who replaces Padre Damaso. Despite being put in place to replace an evil man, he proves to be an even bigger danger than his predecessor.
DON RAFAEL IBARRA
Crisostomo's father who called out the Spanish priests, and so caught the anger of Padre Damaso. Accused of being an enemy of the church, he is imprisoned, dying behind bars. His remains were buried in the Catholic cemetery, but Padre Damaso had his body transferred to the Chinese cemetery.
